Art and Craft Classes

At Shine With Giggles, our Art and Craft classes provide a wonderful platform for children and adults to explore their creative potential. These sessions are thoughtfully designed to inspire imagination, encourage self-expression, and develop essential skills. From colorful paintings and intricate paper crafts to exciting DIY projects, we offer a variety of activities that are both engaging and educational.

Children learn to express their thoughts and emotions through art while developing fine motor skills, focus, and problem-solving abilities. Adults, on the other hand, can rediscover their creativity, relax, and enjoy the satisfaction of creating something beautiful and unique.

  • Drawing and Painting.
  • Origami and Paper Crafts.
  • Clay Modeling and Sculpting.
  • DIY Projects and Decor Ideas.
  • Seasonal and Festival-Themed Crafts.

Vandana Sholapurkar

Vandana Sholapurkar, with extensive experience as an Art and Craft teacher in primary schools, daycare, and activity centers, fosters creativity in children through hands-on learning and artistic exploration. She is also the owner of Aarambh Hand Painted Products, where she creates unique, hand-painted items that blend tradition and modern design.
