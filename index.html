Welcome to Shine With Giggles

Where Learning Begins with Laughter!

We are constantly expanding the range of services offered, Welcome to Shine With Giggles Preschool, Daycare, and Activity Centre, a premier early childhood education hub designed to nurture young minds. Founded by Sneha Dixit, an experienced educator with a passion for children, our center is committed to creating a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment for your little ones.

With a team of dedicated and trained professionals, we focus on early childhood education, skill development, and overall well-being to create a strong foundation for every child’s future. Our approach integrates structured learning, free play, and interactive activities that encourage exploration, self-expression, and social-emotional growth.

What Differentiates Us?

We're committed to creating a strong foundation for your child’s bright future in an environment filled with joy, care, and inspiration.

  • Child centric approach rather than teacher-centric
  • Strong Montessori-based philosophy combined with best practices of early childhood methodology.
  • Goes way beyond song & dance routines, nursery rhymes, and simple playing with toys
  • Materials and tools for learning designed for the curriculum
  • A methodology that instill a sense of purpose & interest to work hard in the child
  • Revolutionary assessment technique
  • A structured learning program for the overall development of the child.
